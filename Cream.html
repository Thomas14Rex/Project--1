Factors Affecting Fat % of Cream

  • Position of cream screw ( or skim milk screw)
    • Cream screw IN or skim milk screw OUT , higher fat % in cream
  • Fat percentage in milk
    • The higher the fat% in milk the higher the fat % in cream
  • Speed of the bowl
    • The higher the speed of the bowl the higher the fat % in cream
  • Rate of inflow of milk
    • The higher the rate of milk inflow the lower the fat % in cream
  • Temperature of milk
    • The lower the temperature of milk the higher the fat % in cream 40C is satisfactory temp
  • Amount of water or skim milk added to flush the bowl
    • The greater the quantity of water or skim milk the lower the fat % in cream
